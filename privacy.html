Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how AiRingtone handles information. We focus on minimal, privacy-first design.

1. What We Collect

  • Personal audio is never collected without your explicit action (e.g., when you share an audio file).
  • Optional diagnostics (crash logs, performance metrics) help improve stability.
  • Account or credit-related info may be processed by our server when you purchase credits, solely to provide services.

2. How We Use Data

  • Provide ringtone generation, editing, and sync features you request.
  • Improve app quality and troubleshoot issues.
  • Comply with legal obligations where required.

3. Storage & Security

We employ industry-standard measures to protect data in transit and at rest. Audio you edit locally stays on-device unless you choose to upload or share.

4. Third Parties

We may integrate trusted services (e.g., App Store, payment/receipt verification). They receive only the minimum required information.

5. Your Choices

  • You can opt out of diagnostics in system settings.
  • You may request deletion of server-side account/credit data via email.

6. Contact

For privacy questions or requests, email: junhua.qian@gmail.com

This policy may be updated from time to time. Material changes will be reflected on this page.
